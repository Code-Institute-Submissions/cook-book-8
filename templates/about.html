Meet the Chef

Dan is a British chef, restaurateur, writer and television personality.

His restaurants have been awarded 16 Michelin stars in total and currently hold a total of seven. His signature restaurant, Restaurant Dan in Chelsea, London, has held three Michelin stars since 2001. After rising to fame on the British television miniseries Boiling Point in 1998

Dan had become one of the best-known and most influential chefs in the UK by 2004
