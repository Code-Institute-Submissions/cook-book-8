FAQ

  • How do I add a meal?
    To add a meal, you must fill in the form through this link HERE. You can also find the Add Meal button at the top of every page and if on a mobile device or tablet, through the 'Burger' button (3 Horizontal Lines) or Via the Green ADD MEAL button found on the home page.
  • How do I edit a meal?
    You can press any meal on the HOME page, this will show you a short description of the meal with a button (Orange, Pen Symbol), press this button to edit and save the meal under a new name/same name.
  • How do I delete a meal?
    You can press any meal on the HOME page, this will show you a short description of the meal with a button (Red, Bin Symbol), press this button and you will be taken to another page of which you can confirm the deletion of the meal.
  • Can I include brand names in my meals?
    You can choose to use brand names when submitting a meal, however please attempt to avoid the use of any Trademarked recipe titles.
  • Can I include recipes from newspapers etc?
    Yes you can, so long as the recipe is not Trademarked.
  • Will you be editing our recipes?
    No, we will not interfere unless your recipe name etc, contains profanity, hate-speech, Trademarked titles or any obvious errors.
